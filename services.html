Services

Our one-stop solution for premium pet care

Physical and Logistic Services

Our logistics team operates a rapid, fully equipped mobile ambulance. As soon as a report is validated on our website,the unit deploys to provide on-site first aid and ensure safe transport to our headquarters

Cat Care

We offer specialized healthcare for cats. This includes routine vaccinations, emergency virus treatments, and systematic sterilization protocols to ethically control the stray cat population.

Veterinary Clinic

Our state-of-the-art medical center is open to the public for professional pet care. Every paid treatment or vaccine for your home pet directly helps fund our stray animal rescue operations.

Veterinary Care

Get access to top-tier medical expertise for your loyal companions. We handle everything from routine wellness check-ups and preventative medicine to advanced diagnostics and critical care.

Health & Treatment

We manage complex medical cases, including emergency trauma surgeries, fracture stabilization, and post-operative isolation zones to ensure a safe,sterile recovery for all animals.

Smart Urban Stations

We deploy solar-powered urban stations providing clean food and water. Through our website, citizens can monitor station status, track animal health, and make targeted donations for specific medical cases.
